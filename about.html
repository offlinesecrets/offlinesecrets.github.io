Why to store secrets offline?
Storing secrets offline means having them only in the physical form. There is no way to obtain them via activities related to computing. Therefore, there is no way the secret can be leaked or hacked.
How to make sure my secrets are not sent to any server?
Download the page using File > Save Page As..., disconnect the Internet and then open the page from the local file.
Why use QR codes to store encrypted data?
It is easier to scan a QR code than to type the long text manually.
